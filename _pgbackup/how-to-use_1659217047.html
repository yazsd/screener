- About Us -
We are a team of like-minded traders. The desire to achieve optimal results in trading in the financial markets has united us.



Trading signals are ideas for a certain pair produced by our team, which are broken down into suggested compounded entries and targets to either buy or sell based on Trading Analysis. Our suggested trades are based on Trading analyses like S/R, patterns, and technical indicators. Whenever possible, given our 24/7 monitoring of news, they will also are supported with Fundamental Analysis.


Using services that help track large densities (volumes) in the spot and futures markets on Binance adds confidence and accuracy to our strategies. We can identify buy and sell orders, number of coins, volume, volatility, number of approaches to the order, and much more, which will help us to correctly assess the possibility and potential of the transaction.
